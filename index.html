Visual analytics of movement data

Find position, time, extent and duration of traffic jams in San Francisco bay area

http://traffic.berkeley.edu/project/downloads/mobilecenturydata

http://research.microsoft.com/en-us/downloads/b16d359d-d164-469e-9fd4-daa38f2b2e13/default.aspx

Visualization of movement trajectories, taking velocity and acceleration of movement into account
